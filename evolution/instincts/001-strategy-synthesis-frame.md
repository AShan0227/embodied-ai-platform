# Strategy Synthesis Frame

For strategic ecosystem research, use a fixed synthesis frame:
1. Signal scan by platform/vendor
2. Extract shared constraints and convergence points
3. Derive meta-paradigms and board-level implications
4. Add confidence tiering to distinguish strategic priors from watch-and-wait
